Checkpoint 4.3

Identify the primary natural language of a document.

The language of the document must be specified in the HTML expression. This example, the main language of the content is Italian.

<html lang=”it”>

<head></head>

<body>

<p>Volete sapere l’origine della parola cappuccino,

il popolare caffelatte all’italiana?</p>

</body>

</html>
