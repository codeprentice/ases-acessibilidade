Checkpoint 10.5

Until user agents (including assistive technologies) render adjacent links distinctly, include non-link, printable characters (surrounded by spaces) between adjacent links.

Example:

[ <a href=”a.htm”>Section A</a>|

<a href=”b.htm”>Section B</a> |

<a href=”c.htm”>Section C</a> |

<a href=”d.htm”>Section D</a> |

<a href=”e.htm”>Section E</a> ]

This way, when the screen reader is in use, there will be a space time among the links due to the non-linked characteres among them.
