Checkpoint 10.4

Until user agents handle empty controls correctly, include default, place-holding characters in edit boxes and text areas.

For example, in HTML, do this for TEXTAREA and INPUT.

A solution example:

Your name <input type=”text” name=”Name” size=”50” value=”*“ /> Comment<textarea name=”TextArea1” rows=”4” cols=”50”> Write your comments here: </textarea>
<input type=”submit” value=”Send this form.”/>
