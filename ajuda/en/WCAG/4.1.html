Checkpoint 4.1

Clearly identify changes in the natural language of a document's text and any (e.g., captions).

Use the LANG atributo to identify the modifications of the language in the text.

Example:

Mother, he told me to go.

He said, “Allons, Madame plaisante!”

And the code should be:

Mother, he told ne to go.

He said, <span lang=”fr”> “Allons, Madame plaisante!”</span>
